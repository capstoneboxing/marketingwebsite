The Need
To Knows

01. Perfect Boxer

We define a theoretical perfect boxer with maximum scores (10/10) across all 20 performance attributes in 5 categories. This upper-bound benchmark evaluates and ranks real fighters objectively.

02. Weighted Scoring

Attributes are grouped into 5 categories — Performance Metrics (35%), Technical (22%), Tactical (16%), Anthropometric (15%) and Psychological (12%) — weighted by their proven impact on fight outcomes.

03. Score Calculation

A fighter's overall score is computed as the weighted sum of their attribute ratings, producing a single comparable metric out of 10 that reflects their true competitive standing.

04. Prediction Engine

Score differences between two fighters are fed through a sigmoid function to produce win probabilities. A base draw probability is factored in to account for closely matched fighters.
