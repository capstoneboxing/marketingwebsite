Step 01
Select Your Fighters

Select a Weight Class and Insert two boxers full names you want to compare. Click Generate to retrieve their attributes.

Step 02
Review Attribute Scores

Inspect each fighter's weighted scores across all 5 categories. Make Adjustments where you deem necessary.

Step 03
Get Your Prediction

Select Calculate Prediction and Receive AI-generated win probabilities with full transparency on the reasoning.

01
Choose Two Fighters

Browse and select any two fighters from our curated database of professional boxers. Each fighter profile contains verified statistics across 20 performance attributes, sourced from official fight records and updated monthly.

Selection
02
Weighted Scoring System Activates

Our engine scores each fighter against a theoretical "perfect boxer" benchmark. Attributes are grouped into five weighted categories: Performance Metrics (35%), Technical Skills (22%), Tactical Ability (16%), Anthropometric Data (15%) and Psychological Factors (12%). Each attribute is rated out of 10.

Calculation
03
Composite Score Comparison

The weighted sum of all attribute scores produces the predicted win/lose probability of the match. You can explore the attribute comparison as well as dive into Prediction Explanations — understanding exactly where one fighter has an edge, score difference interpretation and a quick reminder of uncertainty.

Analysis
04
Sigmoid Prediction Engine Delivers Results

The score differential between fighters is run through a sigmoid function to convert it into a win probability percentage. A base draw probability is also factored in — ensuring close matchups reflect real-world unpredictability. The result: a clear, data-backed prediction showing who is likely to win and by what margin.

Prediction
◈
Transparent Methodology

No black boxes. Every score, weight and probability is visible — so you understand the reasoning behind each prediction.

◎
Objective Rankings

Fighters are measured against a universal "perfect boxer" benchmark, eliminating bias from era, promotion, or fame.

◇
Multi-Dimensional Analysis

20 attributes across 5 categories go beyond win/loss records to capture technical skill, physical traits, ring IQ and mental resilience.

▲
Monthly Data Updates

Fighter data is refreshed monthly to reflect recent bouts, form changes and new performance metrics.
